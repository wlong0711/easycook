## Getting Started

1. **Download & Install**
   - Clone this repository or download the latest release.
   - Install dependencies by running:
     ```sh
     flutter pub get
     ```
   - Run the app using:
     ```sh
     flutter run
     ```
   - For mobile, build and install the APK or run on an emulator.

2. **Sign Up / Log In**
   - Open the app and create a new account or log in with your existing credentials.

## Features

- **Recipe Search:** Find recipes by ingredients, cuisine, or dietary preferences.
- **Meal Planner:** Plan your meals for the week and generate shopping lists.
- **Pantry Management:** Keep track of ingredients you have at home.
- **Shopping List:** Create and manage your grocery shopping list.
- **Favorites & Folders:** Save your favorite recipes and organize them into folders.
- **Profile Management:** Edit your profile and view your cooking stats.

## How to Use

### Authentication
- **Sign Up:** Enter your email, password, and any required details to create an account.
- **Log In:** Use your credentials to access your account.
- **Forgot Password:** Use the "Forgot Password" link to reset your password.

### Recipe Search
- Use the search bar to find recipes by name, ingredient, or cuisine.
- Filter results by dietary needs (e.g., vegetarian, gluten-free).
- Tap a recipe to view details, ingredients, and instructions.

### Meal Planner
- Access the meal planner from the main menu.
- Add recipes to specific days/meals.
- View your weekly plan and adjust as needed.

### Pantry Management
- Add items to your pantry manually or by scanning ingredients.
- Update quantities as you use or purchase items.
- The app can suggest recipes based on your pantry contents.

### Shopping List
- Add items manually or generate a list from your meal plan.
- Check off items as you shop.
- Remove purchased items from the list.

### Profile & Favorites
- View and edit your profile information.
- Save recipes to your favorites for quick access.
- Organize favorites into custom folders.
